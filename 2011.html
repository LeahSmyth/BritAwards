2011

Fashion designer Dame Vivienne Westwood gave the 2011 Brit award a fresh look.


“We are honoured she accepted our offer of designing 2011's trophy,” said Brits chairman David Joseph.


Dame Vivienne’s design made the 2011 trophy will be a more colourful affair than the customary silver statuette.


According to Joseph, the 69-year-old “stands for all the things we want to inspire in tomorrow’s generation of artists. Dame Vivienne continues to be unique, innovative and not to mention massively inspired by music and vice versa.”


Westwood is one of the best-known and original designers in the international fashion business, she made her name defining the look of punk rebellion in the 1970s.
